Prusa Foundation

Prusa Art, Cultural and Education Foundation has aimed to provide educational and cultural services to Turkish and American living in United States.

  • Literature

Ebru

Ebru is the traditional art of creating colorful patterns by sprinkling and brushing color pigments on a pan of oily water.

Horon

Horon folk dance is a fast-paced dance practiced especially in the Black Sea region of Turkey.
